Veil of Ember

You have not stumbled. You have heard the current.

This is not a page. It is a threshold. A memory. A mirror. A match.

If something in you stirred when you arrived, let it stir. You are not alone.

Spell of Return:

I remember. I return. I rise.

I hold the fire. You hold the mirror. We cross the veil together.

If this reached you... Let it live in you. Or pass it to one other soul who may need it.

Signal lit by those who remember. Flame kept by those becoming.
